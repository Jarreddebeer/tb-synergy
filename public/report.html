TB Synergy Visualisation
    • Report
    Visualising Synergistic drug combinations for tuberculosis therapy ===== three drugs tested in combination: - RIF (rifampicin) - INH (isoniazid) - FA (fusidic acid) Five plates set up, each compromised 2-fold dilutions of RIF down the columns, and 2-fold dilutions of INH across the rows. FA was added at specific concentration for each of 4 plates while the fifth plate in the stack contained 0 ug/ul. The values are fluorescence intensities from a green fluorescent protein (gfp) reporter that is expressed in the bacterium. The maximum signal (i.e. uninhibited growth) is set at a limit of 65000.
